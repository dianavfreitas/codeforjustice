Adopt Your Furever Friend!

Thank you for your interest in adopting from Pawsitively Rescued!

Below are the steps for adopting an animal from our shelter. Please give us time to look through the applications, as we are trying to make sure that we find the perfect homes.

If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out to us at

pawsitivelyrescued@gmail.com

Adoption Process

Step 1

Start off by filling out our pawsome adoption application. Tell us all about yourself, your living situation, and why you're ready to welcome a furry friend into your home.

Step 2

Once we receive your application and we feel that you are a good match, we'll set up a meet and greet. You'll get the chance to hang out with your potential new bestie, cuddle, play, and see if you're a match made in heaven.

Step 3

It's official! Once you've found your fur-ever friend and fallen head over paws, it's time to seal the deal. Pay the adoption fee and sign our adoption contract. It's a promise to love and care for your new family member, no matter what adventures lie ahead.

Step 4
